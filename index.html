✈️ Airport Safety Tips

Stay alert, stay safe — make every flight smooth and stress-free.

1. Keep Your Luggage Close

Never leave your bags unattended. Unattended luggage can be stolen or flagged by security, causing delays and safety concerns.

2. Have ID and Documents Ready

Keep your passport, boarding pass, and ID accessible. Being prepared keeps lines moving and reduces the risk of losing important papers.

3. Follow Security Instructions

Listen carefully to TSA or airport personnel. Clear communication helps keep everyone safe and ensures a smoother process.

4. Stay Aware of Your Surroundings

Report any suspicious activity and keep an eye out for your belongings. Awareness is key in large public places like airports.

5. Take Care of Yourself

Drink water, get rest, and manage stress. Staying healthy and calm helps you make better decisions during travel.
