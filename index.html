✈️ Airport Safety Tips

B

1. Watch Your Belongings

Keep your bags close and never leave them unattended. Airports are busy places, and unattended items can be stolen or flagged as a security concern.

2. Be Ready for Security

Have your boarding pass and ID ready, and follow TSA rules. This makes the process faster and helps avoid stress or holding up the line.

3. Stay Close to Your Gate

Gate changes and early boarding calls happen often. Staying nearby prevents you from missing important announcements.

4. Keep Important Documents Safe

Store your passport, ID, and tickets in a secure travel pouch. Losing them can delay your travel or prevent boarding entirely.

5. Be Aware of Your Surroundings

Keep an eye on people around you, especially in crowded areas like food courts or baggage claim, to avoid scams or theft.

6. Know Emergency Exits

When you arrive, locate the nearest exits and security personnel. In case of an emergency, you’ll know exactly where to go.
