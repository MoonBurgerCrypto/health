✈️ Airport Safety Tips

By Alex Gochenaur

1. Watch Your Belongings

Keep your bags close and never leave them unattended. Airports are busy places, and unattended items can be stolen or flagged as a security concern.

2. Be Ready for Security

Have your boarding pass and ID ready, and follow rules to make security smoother for everyone. This helps avoid stress and keeps lines moving quickly.

3. Follow Staff Instructions

Always listen to airport staff and follow safety instructions. They’re trained to keep everyone safe and make sure flights stay on schedule.

4. Practice Good Hygiene

Wash your hands often and use sanitizer after touching surfaces like escalator rails or trays. This helps you stay healthy while traveling.

5. Stay Seated and Buckled

Once you’re on the plane, keep your seatbelt fastened when seated. Turbulence can happen anytime, so staying buckled keeps you safe.

✈️ Stay alert, stay safe, and enjoy your travels!
