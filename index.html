✈️ Airport Safety Tips

By Alex Gochenaur

1. Watch Your Belongings

Keep your bags close and never leave them unattended. Unattended items can be stolen or flagged as security concerns.

2. Be Ready for Security

Have your boarding pass and ID ready, remove liquids/laptops when required, and follow posted rules to keep lines moving.

3. Follow Staff Instructions

Airport staff are trained to keep everyone safe — follow directions, signage, and announcements to help flights run smoothly.

4. Practice Good Hygiene

Wash hands often and use sanitizer after touching public surfaces like rails or security trays to stay healthy while traveling.

5. Stay Seated and Buckled

Keep your seatbelt fastened while seated — turbulence can happen unexpectedly, and buckling keeps you safe.

✈️ Stay alert, stay safe, and enjoy your travels!
